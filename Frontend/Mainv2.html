About FarmEye

Learn more about our satellite analytics platform

Satellite Technology

FarmEye utilizes advanced satellite imagery from multiple sources including Landsat, Sentinel, and commercial providers to deliver high-resolution agricultural insights. Our platform processes multispectral data to analyze crop health, soil conditions, and environmental factors.

AI Processing

Our machine learning algorithms analyze satellite data using computer vision and deep learning techniques. The system can detect crop stress, predict yields, monitor irrigation efficiency, and identify pest or disease outbreaks with high accuracy.

Analysis Options

Low Accuracy: Quick analysis using basic algorithms - suitable for general monitoring.
Medium Accuracy: Balanced processing with advanced AI - recommended for most agricultural applications.
High Accuracy: Maximum precision using ensemble models and temporal analysis - ideal for critical decisions.

Processing Times

Analysis times vary based on selected accuracy level, area size, and current server load. You'll receive email and SMS notifications when your analysis is complete. All processing is done on secure cloud infrastructure with enterprise-grade security.
